# The ROSCA Path — argument spine (v1)
_Riften Labs pitch deck · structural rebuild · 2026-07-05_

**Rule:** the story is the ARGUMENT, not a character. Every beat forces the next
(but/therefore, never and-then). The protagonist is the idea.

**The pitch in one sentence:**
Stablecoins can bank 1.4 billion people — but only if ordinary people can safely
hold a private key, and the savings circle that predates banking in every poor
community on earth is the trust layer that finally makes that possible.

## I. THE PRIZE & THE WALL
1. **The prize** — $322B stable dollars, 1.4B unbanked, ~0 connection. The win: a
   dollar that doesn't inflate, on a phone they already own. [old 4]
2. **The one wall** — not supply / phones / literacy. Holding digital dollars =
   holding a key: 12 words, lose them → gone forever; hand them to a company →
   you just rebuilt the bank. Name the obstacle ONCE, here. [old 1, 5, 6]

## II. WHY NO ONE HAS CROSSED IT
3. **15 years of failure** — two dead approaches: teach everyone to hold a key
   (still a hobby for the technical) + social recovery (the *right* idea, but it
   rusts: the quorum decays, and it's never practiced). [old 7, 8, 9]
4. **THE HINGE** — social recovery has never had one thing: a trust group that
   stays intact AND stays rehearsed, on a schedule. Biggest, barest moment in the
   deck. [old 10, promoted from a throwaway]

## III. THE TURN — WHAT WAS ALREADY THERE
5. **Older than the bank** — "bank" is a loanword everywhere; the institution was
   imported. What came first has a native name in every culture (susu, esusu,
   hui, chit, kameti, tanda…): the ROSCA — 10–20 neighbors, a rotating pot, a
   fixed meeting, self-enforcing, 1,000 years old, hundreds of millions. [old 11–14]
6. **The click** — the ROSCA *is* the missing piece: the circle is the quorum
   that never decays (the pot enforces attendance); the meeting is the practice.
   Social recovery's two failure modes are the two things a ROSCA already solves.
   [old 15–18, as argument]

## IV. WHAT WE BUILD
7. **The mechanism** — turn the circle into the recovery ring: the meeting becomes
   a key ceremony, the members the guardians, the ritual the drill. 12 in the
   circle, 5 hold shares, any 3 restore. [old 17, 19]
8. **The product** — the ROSCA app: the circle they already run, recovery native
   to it. [old 22, de-jargoned]

## V. WHY US & WHY IT'S A BUSINESS  (the real climax)
9. **The unfair advantage** — we supply the two things the circle can't make
   itself: MUSD, a dollar held without a bank (minted non-custodially — we mint
   it, we never hold it) + sub-cent rails. [old 23, expanded — jargon paid off]
10. **A company, not a charity** — circles are distribution we don't pay for (1
    circle = 12 users at once); contributions are standing dollar demand; every
    member arrives *already trained* in self-custody → zero CAC for the exact
    behavior the industry burns money failing to teach. [old 24, promoted to payoff]

## VI. OBJECTIONS & THE ASK
11. **What breaks** — collusion (pot < the reputation it burns), fraying (the key
    never leaves him), the freeze (the room won't let him fail), regulators
    (non-custodial: nothing to seize, nothing to license). [old 25]
12. **The ask** — 10 circles in Lagos, 90 days, one metric: the first lost phone
    restored in a church hall, nothing missing. [old 26]
13. **The close** — don't build a bank, don't build a wallet; ride the trust
    that's already there to carry a genuinely new technology. [old 27]

## What this fixes (the five snags)
- The wall is named once (2), not four times.
- The hinge (4) is the biggest moment, not the smallest.
- The climax moved from Tunde's lost phone → the business (9–10).
- Substance (product / moat / economics) is a movement, not a crammed afterthought.
- Tunde's contradiction is gone — the argument no longer needs him to carry it.

## Decisions made (overrule freely)
- **Character-free.** The spine stands with zero named people; it rests on the
  TRUE claim (these people already run sophisticated trust infrastructure), not
  the victim version that fought the pitch. One optional human beat is available
  at beat 6 (the click) or as proof-of-restore under beat 8 — earned, cuttable,
  no manufactured pathos. Build it character-free first; add a face only if it
  wants one.
- **Beat 5 keeps the "what was there first" turn** — but as a straight claim, not
  a teased mystery.

~13–15 slides, down from 27. The tightening *is* the smoothness.
