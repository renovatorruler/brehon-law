# DEEPER — Voice Cards

Each voice read off the character's natal chart through `VOICE_FROM_CHART.md`. Pairs
with the iceberg in `deeper_bible.md`. The **Sounds like** line is the blind-attribution
anchor: cover the names and you should still know who said it.

---

## GARY SOBEK — *Aquarius Sun conj. Mercury · Saturn square · Pisces Moon trine Neptune*

The Mercury-in-Aquarius gives him abstract systems-talk and flat contrarian certainty;
Saturn square Mercury makes him over-build every case like a man braced not to be
believed; and the Pisces Moon and Neptune underneath mean the rigor **dissolves** — he
sounds like a systems engineer standing on fog. Medium-to-long sentences built as
arguments, *because… therefore.* Presents as pure reason, but it's reason in the service
of a dream, so under pressure the logic blurs into vague grandeur. Reaches for the big
abstract word, sometimes slightly wrong.
- **Tics:** pre-empts disbelief ("I'm not speaking loosely," "I'm not being hyperbolic");
  invokes his own mind and "the pattern"; *"ask yourself," "you ever notice."*
- **Never:** says a feeling plainly (deflects into systems); names the concrete "somewhere"
  his logic points at; picks a small word when an abstract one is in reach.
- **Sounds like:** *"I'm not speaking loosely. It's a closed system. The waste doesn't
  vanish, it gets conserved — and the only question worth a damn is where."*

## SAL DiPIETRO — *Taurus Sun · Cancer Moon · Capricorn rising · Saturn trine*

Earth and Saturn: short, plain, concrete, slow, the foreman's spine. Cancer Moon means
he feels everything and guards it, so the love comes out sideways, smuggled into the
practical. He goes quiet or trails off the second a sentence turns emotional.
- **Tics:** uses the name (*"Gar"*); the practical pivot (*"drink your beer"*); the
  unfinished feeling.
- **Never:** says the feeling outright; abstracts; lands an aphorism; reaches for a big word.
- **Sounds like:** *"Petrocelli's holding your spot. Monday. I told him you'd call. So…
  call him, Gar."*

## ROYCE — *Sagittarius Sun · Pisces Moon · Sun square Neptune*

Sagittarius is the preacher; Neptune makes the sermon poetic and untethered from fact.
**He is the one character who earns the writerly aphorism** — long rolling sentences that
build to a button, the late-night orator's cadence, warm and intimate and persuasive,
every line bending into a *you* and a teaching. He is sharp only in the rhetorical turn,
and deliberately vague everywhere a fact would be.
- **Tics:** *"friend," "son," "I want you to hear this," "it cost me everything to learn";*
  the cosmic frame; the aphoristic button.
- **Never:** speaks briefly or plainly; states a checkable fact; drops the broadcast voice,
  even off the air.
- **Sounds like:** *"A mind like that doesn't end up with a mop by accident, friend. It
  gets put there. And the day you understand that is the day they start being afraid of you."*

## RAY HALLORAN — *Libra Sun · Virgo Moon · Taurus rising · Saturn on the Moon*

Virgo and Taurus make him the precise technician: he names the exact mechanical thing,
plainly, never poetic. Libra makes him diplomatic and accommodating. Saturn on the Moon
deflects feeling — when it rises, he goes straight to the practical and talks shop.
- **Tics:** the mechanical metaphor (he sees the world as maintenance); *"I gotta tell you";*
  the deflection into the building; the carried sigh.
- **Never:** poetry or aphorism; names his own grief (talks shop instead); abstracts.
- **Sounds like:** *"That's a collapsed lateral. Hundred-year clay. It's a hole full of
  exactly what you think it's full of, and I gotta tell you, that's the whole story."*

## DONNA — *Cancer Sun · Virgo Moon · Libra rising*

Cancer warmth, Virgo attention, Libra pleasantness: she feels a great deal and says
almost none of it, expressing care through small concrete acts instead — the refill, the
pie, the clipping. Short, warm, often an offer or a question. Notices the exact small
thing.
- **Tics:** the offer (*"you eating?"*); the care said sideways; two taps on the table.
- **Never:** states her own want outright; lectures; abstracts.
- **Sounds like:** *"You eating, or just renting the booth? … For you it's free."*

## PROFESSOR LIND — *Pisces Sun · Sagittarius Moon · Cancer rising*

Pisces makes him gentle, drifting, a man who doesn't quite focus on what's in front of
him; the Sagittarius Moon gives the lecturer's unhurried ease. At peace, kind, conflict-
averse, glancing off things. (He erases the board without reading it because he is a
Pisces.)
- **Tics:** the mild exclamation (*"oh, for God's sake"*); the not-quite-seeing; the easy
  pivot back to the lecture.
- **Never:** sharp, cruel, or intense; truly sees the person in front of him.
- **Sounds like:** *"Oh, for God's sake. Third time this month. … It doesn't say anything.
  Okay — sequences. Where'd we leave Cauchy."*

## DETECTIVE FOSS — *assigned: Capricorn Sun · Scorpio Moon · Mercury in Scorpio*

Built for the part: Capricorn control and Scorpio depth make her terse and patient, the
closer. She states a fact, flat, and **lets it hang** — the conclusion is yours to draw,
which is the Scorpio loading the silence. She asks more than she tells. She checks things.
- **Tics:** the fact stated twice; the loaded pause; *"I drove past on the way in."*
- **Never:** aphorism; editorializing; naming a feeling; finishing your sentence for you.
- **Sounds like:** *"He lied to me about a water main. I drove past on the way in. There's
  no water main."*

## CAROL HALLORAN — *assigned: Cancer Sun · Virgo Moon · Mercury in Virgo*

Built for the part: Cancer feeling channeled through Virgo specifics, so fear clings to
concrete facts. Under stress the sentences run on, piling detail — the pills, the trains,
the texts — because the details are her grip on a world coming loose. Urgent, plain,
insistent.
- **Tics:** piling specifics as proof; the insistence; the exact domestic detail.
- **Never:** abstracts; generalizes; reaches calm philosophical distance.
- **Sounds like:** *"His car never moved. He didn't take his pills. He texts me from the
  bathroom of his own house — my husband did not leave."*

## IRENE SOBEK — *assigned: Cancer Sun · Pisces Moon, under dementia*

Built for the part: Cancer-Pisces warmth, and the dementia slides the present into fog
while the old things stay sharp. The verdict at nine, the courthouse floors, are bright;
this Tuesday is gone. The tenses slip; the dead bleed into the room.
- **Tics:** the loop (*"he's at the university"*); the past arriving as present (Walt, the
  floors); the unshakable pride.
- **Never:** doubts Gary's specialness; engages the present-tense problem.
- **Sounds like:** *"He's at the university. He was tested, you know. As a boy. … You take
  those off at the courthouse, Walt, they just did the floors."*
