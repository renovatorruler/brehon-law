ACTION: A hand finds the pull-cord in the dark and the light comes on with a CLICK.
ACTION: The bulb swings over an APOLLO PROCEDURES TRAINER built into the far end of the barn.
ACTION: Hand-machined gauges crowd the panel, each dial cut and numbered by hand.
ACTION: A crew couch sits bolted into a welded steel cradle in front of the panel.
ACTION: A triangular window faces a Moon painted on the barn boards, gray and flat.
ACTION: ALONG THE BACK WALL -- spiral notebooks stand floor to roof, every spine dated in the same block hand.
ACTION: The bottom shelf starts in 1972.
ACTION: CRICKET, 79, in a barn coat, lowers himself onto the couch and lifts a hand microphone off its hook.
ACTION: He keys the mic.
CRICKET: Net's open. Tuesday, nineteen-oh-two local.
ACTION: The speaker answers first in a flat, dry voice.
DUTCH (RADIO): Dutch is up. Got you loud.
ACTION: A slow Texas drawl comes on behind him.
STITCH (RADIO): Stitch is here. Settled in, coffee's hot, y'all take your time.
ACTION: The last voice is clipped and Navy.
GUNNY (RADIO): Tibbs, up and on time. Which is better than Texas managed.
GUNNY (RADIO): It's nineteen-oh-three, gentlemen. Commander, run your checklist.
ACTION: Cricket props a plastic card against the panel and works down it with one finger.
CRICKET: Starting down.
ACTION: He throws a row of toggles and the panel lights shift.
ACTION: A needle on the panel drifts off its mark.
ACTION: The buzz behind the panel stutters, drops, catches again.
CRICKET: Needle's walking. Low left.
DUTCH (RADIO): Steady, or does it stutter?
CRICKET: Stutters.
DUTCH (RADIO): That's Bus B. Same relay I put in in eighty-four.
STITCH (RADIO): Here he goes.
DUTCH (RADIO): I'm allowed to say it. Kick it, low left corner.
ACTION: Cricket lifts his boot and raps the lower left corner of the panel with his heel.
ACTION: The needle climbs back onto its mark and the buzz behind the panel smooths out.
ACTION: He eases a lever down a notch and watches the altimeter unwind.
ACTION: A blue lamp lights on the panel.
CRICKET: Contact light. Engine stop.
ACTION: He flips two switches and the buzz dies out.
ACTION: For a moment nobody keys a mic and the barn holds still.
STITCH (RADIO): Hair late on the throttle there at the end.
GUNNY (RADIO): The man is on the ground, Colonel.
CRICKET: Net closed. Same time Tuesday.
GUNNY (RADIO): God willing.
ACTION: Cricket presses the key twice, two short clicks.
ACTION: Two clicks come back over the speaker. Then two more behind them.
ACTION: He hangs the microphone on its hook and opens a fresh spiral notebook.
ACTION: He writes in ballpoint block letters, and the page reads T+2,806. BUS B CORRECTED. CONTACT 19:41. CREW PRESENT BY RADIO.
ACTION: He runs a thumb down the dated spines, from 1972 up to the shelf by his head.
ACTION: He pulls the cord and the barn goes dark, then slides the door shut behind him.
ACTION: He sets a padlock through the hasp and tests it with one pull.
ACTION: Across the yard the farmhouse porch light burns over the steps.
ACTION: Cricket crosses to it. No lights are on inside the house.
ACTION: He climbs the steps and lets himself in.
