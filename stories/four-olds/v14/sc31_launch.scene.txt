ACTION: The rocket stands white in the floodlight against the black.
ACTION: Vapor rolls down the hull and spreads out across the cold concrete.
ACTION: No mark rides the side of it. The skin is white from the nose to the base.
ACTION: The floods hold the pad. The steam keeps coming off the metal.
ACTION: Inside the gala hall the tables run in long rows under warm light.
ACTION: A scale model of the rocket sits under glass at the center of the room. A small brass plate on the base reads HARMONY BANNER.
ACTION: PRESIDENT ZORAN MARWANI, 41, works the tables with a glass in one hand and a hand for every shoulder he passes.
ACTION: He stops at a table near the front and crouches down to a seated guest.
MARWANI: There you are. No, don't get up, please, you came all this way for us.
MARWANI: How's the boy. He still building the little ones out of the soup cans.
ACTION: He laughs at the answer and pats the man twice on the arm and stands.
ACTION: A wide barn-door screen fills the wall behind the riser with the pad and the standing rocket.
ACTION: He steps up onto the low riser and taps the side of his glass with his ring.
MARWANI: Thank you. Everybody, sit down, please. I'll keep it short. I always say that and I never do it, so.
MARWANI: Tonight a rocket rises with no flag on its side. Think of that. Not conquest. Not pride. Repair.
ACTION: Inside mission control the screens light the faces along the consoles.
ACTION: MIRA VESS, 40s, stands at the back glass with her arms crossed and her eyes on the count clock.
ACTION: ADMINISTRATOR J. PELL, 50s, stands a few feet down the same glass with a folder held flat to his chest.
ACTION: A flight director leans to his mic at the front console.
FLIGHT DIRECTOR: All stations, we're inside two minutes. Give me a go or a no-go for the launch.
CONTROLLER (RADIO): Fuel's good here, we're go.
CONTROLLER (RADIO): Guidance is go.
CONTROLLER (RADIO): Range is go, you're clear.
ACTION: Vess does not move. Her eyes stay on the clock.
ACTION: Pell opens the folder, runs a finger down one line, and closes it again.
ACTION: Inside the sealed crate the padded walls press in close around a man on his back.
ACTION: EARL 'CRICKET' DAWES, 79, lies back with a checklist card clipped to the front of his suit.
ACTION: A low hum runs up through the walls of the crate.
ACTION: The hum climbs. Dust ticks off the seam of the lid in the amber light.
CRICKET: Straps tight. Card's set.
CRICKET: Hum's up. Right on the minute.
ACTION: Back at the pad the rocket still stands white in the floods.
ACTION: FIRE pours out from under the rocket and the night goes orange for a mile.
ACTION: The roar reaches the gala hall a second late and rattles the glasses on the tables.
ACTION: The rocket lifts off the pad and climbs up into the dark.
ACTION: ON THE GROUND CAMERAS --
ACTION: The white skin of the booster begins to peel back on the climb. It comes off in burning sheets along the length of it.
ACTION: Color shows through where the white is gone. Red and white and blue rise up through the scorch.
ACTION: ON THE GALA SCREEN --
ACTION: The color climbs across the big screen above the room.
ACTION: The raised glasses hang in the air and nobody drinks.
ACTION: Marwani keeps the smile and holds his glass up to a room that has turned away from him to the screen.
ACTION: Inside Roy's Diner the late crowd fills the counter under the TV.
ACTION: BUCK, 70s, sits at the counter in a feed cap with his eyes up on the TV over the pie case.
BUCK: There she goes in a minute. Ours. Whatever's riding her.
ACTION: On the diner TV the color breaks up through the white.
ACTION: The whole counter comes off its stools at once. Nobody says a word.
ACTION: WITH CRICKET --
ACTION: The weight takes him down deep into the molding of the crate. He lays his hand flat on the underside of the lid and holds it there.
ACTION: The amber light dims down under the load. It holds low, and then it comes back up.

ACTION: The lower piece of the stack falls away from the top. The upper part lights and pulls ahead of it.

ACTION: Steam rises off the scorched red and white and blue of its skin.
ACTION: The ground crew stand at the base with their heads tipped back, looking up at it. No one covers it. No one speaks.
