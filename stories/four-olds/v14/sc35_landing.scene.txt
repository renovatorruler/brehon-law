ACTION: The rack slams down against its stops and the straps snap taut out of order.
ACTION: In Crate B a tarped case tears loose, slides half a foot along the deck, and stops hard against the man folded into the far corner.
ACTION: On the cargo door a hinge takes the blow and sets a hair out of true.
ACTION: Outside on the Sea of Tranquility, the dust the landing kicked up hangs a moment and then settles, and across every screen in America the picture goes clean.

ACTION: Back on the flight deck, Lindqvist lets out a breath and reaches up to safe the switches over his head.
LINDQVIST: Well. That's us down, then.

SHEN: Both networks have you.
