# THE FOUR OLDS — production goal (2026-07-08)

**The goal:** a complete feature screenplay — THE FOUR OLDS — written end to
end through every phase of the pipeline, all gates passed, delivered to the
user as final Fountain + PDF. The goal is CLOSED only when the finished script
is in his hands and he calls it complete.

**The deliverable:** ~110–120 page feature, English, Fountain format (plus
rendered PDF), timestamped file naming per convention. Register: rage-and-
release ("make America furious for 30 minutes, let it conspire for an hour,
let it erupt for the rest"). The two laws verified ON THE PAGE: commies named;
the greatness of America in it. Comedy at the apparatus, never at the flags.
Nobody mourns. The finale must goosebump twice.

**Working protocol (binding, from this session's corrections):**
- Right-brain model: I decide execution and bring material; the user judges
  and pivots. No execution questions to him — ever. Premise-level forks only,
  arriving with a committed default.
- His approvals happen at exactly THREE checkpoints (bible, scene map,
  substance draft) — not per scene, not per question.
- Open items (flag condition: leaning vibrant; regime-on-screens balance) are
  MINE to resolve in the bible; he overrules at checkpoint 1 if wrong.
- Substance before finishing (never polish what may be rewritten); finishing
  runs late and fenced.

## PHASES

**PHASE 0 — THE BIBLE.** Consolidate the entire brainstorm (spine v4 + all
amendments, his dictated cold open / Act 1 / Act 2 build / ending, the Vess
flip, the camera/flag resolutions, cast roster with locked names, knowledge
design, act map with minute marks) into ONE authoritative story bible.
Everything prior becomes history; the bible is canon.
*Exit: CHECKPOINT 1 — user reads the bible and approves or pivots.*

**PHASE 1 — THE SCENE MAP.** ~40–45 scenes, each carrying who-wants-what /
what's-in-the-way / what-turns / what-it-costs; A/B weave (the olds' track vs.
the Vess-Hale track vs. the country-as-character track); the held-card
schedule (cookout withhold, Vess reveal, ghost file, fireworks ban payoff);
set-piece placement against the minute map; world pass (full cast on stage).
Structural gate: every scene must turn; corridors get cut or fused.
*Exit: CHECKPOINT 2 — user approves the map.*

**PHASE 2 — THE VOICES.** Voice cards + natal charts (required pass, enforced
by artifact preflight) for: Cricket, Dutch, Stitch, Gunny, the foreman, Joss,
Tito, Hale, Vess, the Monitor, Marwani (screens-only cadence), the son, the
wife. One tic per character; who-earns-eloquence decided; blind-attribution
gate stood up and runnable.
*Exit: cards exist and the blind-attribution harness passes a dry run.*

**PHASE 3 — THE DRAFT.** Every scene written in story order through the gated
pipeline. Substance battery runs per sequence as written: drama (want/wall/
turn on the page, not the outline), clear-pane at the events layer, held
cards held, heart-ledger beats banked, voices distinct. Assembled
continuously into the working Fountain.
*Exit: full draft assembled, every scene gate-passed.*

**PHASE 4 — THE SUBSTANCE READ.** The assembled draft delivered to the user
as a script (Fountain + PDF) for the is-this-the-right-movie read. Rewrite
loop lives here until it is.
*Exit: CHECKPOINT 3 — user substance approval.*

**PHASE 5 — THE FINISHING BATTERY (fenced, post-approval only).** Humanizer /
AI-tell sweep; dialogue realism (echo-tell, appended-fact, messy-speech);
plainness-over-craft; clear-pane line level; the naturalness gate RUN (not
cited); blind-attribution RUN; craftlint zero; trim pass; format lint and
title page.
*Exit: all gates green with receipts.*

**PHASE 6 — DELIVERY.** Final Fountain + PDF, timestamped, sent as files.
Source committed and pushed per the source-only convention.
*Exit: the user has the script and calls it done. Goal closed.*

## DEFINITION OF DONE
1. Complete feature screenplay delivered (Fountain + PDF, timestamped).
2. The two laws demonstrably on the page.
3. Checkpoints 1–3 approved by the user.
4. Substance battery passed per scene; finishing battery passed with receipts
   (naturalness run, blind-attribution run, craftlint 0).
5. Source committed and pushed. Nothing held locally unpushed.
